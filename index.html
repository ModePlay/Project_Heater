Chest Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Free Press 4 12 Use a flat bench. Start at about 10lbs/arm. At top of lift don't fully straighten elbows. *Down slow, up faster. Squeeze chest at top.
Flys 4 12 Start at 5lbs/arm. Keep slight bend in the elbow. Squeeze chest at top and bring weights no closer than 8 inches to maintain resistance.
Pec Deck 4 12 Thought I saw one in your gym. Should be able to sit down and then press bars out. Start light or even pull weight-pin right out to get a feel for it.
Pushups 4 AMRAP Always AMRAP your fist set. Then cut that number in half and thats how many reps to do for the remaining 3 sets. Since you're doing flat bench free press you may want to 'incline' these pushups.

Back Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Lat Pulldowns 4 12 Back straight. Wide grip. Don't think about pulling down with your arms-the arms follow. As you bring your elbows down to your sides, think about and squeeze your back and hold there for just a second.
Bent Over Row 4 12 Back straight. Chin up and head forward. 10lbs/side to start. Same as lats, yes your arm will move, but focus so the majority of the row comes from the back not your arm. If you started with your right side last set, start with your left on the next and so on....
Reverse Flys 4 12 5lbs/arm is plenty to start. Or do it without weight at first to get a feel. Stand with knees slightly bent. Bend at waist so torso is parallel to the ground as can be. Chin up and head forward. Keeping arms straight with a slight bend in your elbows, flair your arms up and out to the sides squeezing your shoulders at the top--that's where you want focus. DON'T try and hold the weight up at the top of the lift. Should be smooth and fluid reps almost like you're flapping wings.
Box Lift 4 12 Safest thing I can think of to target your lower back. Legs slightly more than shoulder-width apart. Arms above your head and imagine a box in your hands. Bend at the waist (push butt out) only so far as keeping your back and pelvis aligned will allow, then let your arms drop further to place the 'box' at your feet. Then push your hips forward as you pick the 'box' back up and overhead. Your legs should not move at all.
Rows 4 12 Try not to arch your back through the motion.

Triceps Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Dips 4 AMRAP First set AMRAP then cut that number in half to get your reps for the remaining 3 sets.
Over Head Kettle Bell 4 12 The single arm over head ones. Using a Kettle bell is prob easier than a dumbell.
Cable Pushdowns 4 12 These are great. Stand and face the seat of the lat-pulldown Then grip the long bar and pull it down to your waist. Now bring it to position where your elbows are tucked an locked to your side, but your forearms are parallel with the ground. Staying straight, not arching your back, push your palms down to your waist. That's one rep. *Don't let your forearms go above parallel until all your reps are done.
Kickbacks 4 12 Start with about 2 1/2lbs per hand. Stand up with slight bend in knees. Bend forward at waist. Chin up and head forward. Pull elbows up so tris are parallel to the ground as you can make them and hold them there (you should almost look like a downhill skiier). Now your reps begin by extending BOTH your forearms out toward the back. Or to say another way, the front of your fist will point from the ground to the back wall for one rep.

Biceps Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Supination Curls 4 12 Palms face in to your sides. Alternate arms. As you lift, gradually twist so your palm faces your shoulder at the top.
Seated Chin-Up 4 12 Use the lat pulldown bar but with a close grip. You can mix it up with your palms facing in or out. Pull to just below your chin, hold there and squeeze your biceps for a second.
W's 4 12 Elbows in and arms flared out in a W. Press your Arms out to the sides but don't go higher than shoulder height and careful not to over-extend.
Cross Body Hammer Curls 4 12 To opposite shoulder and down. Palm should face your body once it leaves your side.
Kettle Bell Curl 4 12 Take a seat with one kettle bell or holding the end of a dumbell between your legs and curl it up.
21's 2 21 **Challenge** (No supination throughout) Start high with weights at your shoulders. Allow them to drop only to your naval 7 times. After the 7th, allow them to drop all the way to your thighs, and then bring them up only to your naval 7 times. After the 7th, complete 7 full range lifts from your thighs to your shoulders.

Leg Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Squat Thrust 4 12 Forward and back, not 'up' and 'down.' *You can do these everyday if you want.
Glute Bridge 4 12 Lie down on your back with knees bent and feet on the mat. Tighten your core and then press into heels and squeeze the glutes to raise your hips upwards, while pushing the upper back into the floor. Hold this position and then return to start to complete 1 rep.
Hamstring Curls 4 12 You can do this with machine or laying flat on your belly. Pull your ankles up to your glutes close as you can. If on your belly, can concentrate on one leg at a time if you wish.
Quad Curls 4 12 Take your time to find a comfortable weight that does not strain your knees. Up slow, squeeze your quads at the top, then down slow.
Super Girl 4 10 per side All fours with knees under hips and hands under shoulders. Bring your left elbow to meet your right knee underneath your torso. Then extend both your arm out and leg back parallel with the floor (fly like Super Girl!) and hold for a second. Once all reps are completed, repeat same on opposite sides.

Shoulder Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Free Weight Shoulder Press 4 12 Suggest to do this seated at first. Find a very light weight to start. You should not feel discomfort in your neck. Arms out to the sides with elbows at shoulder height, press up.
Draw Circles 2 Small to Big to Small Stand up straight arms out to the side like a "T." Keep arms straight the entire time. With your fingertips, draw the tiniest circles in the air as you can, gradually increasing the size until the circles are as big as you can draw, then gradually reduce the size. When the circles are once again small as can be, Repeat in the reverse direction. Take a break and do it all one more time.
Single Arm Raise 4 12 Stand with light weight in each hand with palms facing and resting on your quads. Keeping arm straight with slight bend in elbow, alternate raising the weight to shoulder height right out in front of your chin.
Garfield's 4 12 Light weight in each hand. Palms facing in and hands close together in center as possible. THINK about lifting with your shoulders and then keeping your hands together pull the weights up under your chin (about to your collar bone should be good and comfortable) and hold for 1 second.

Ab Exercises
Exercise Sets Reps *Tips*
Plank 1 AMRAP 30 seconds is your first target. 2 mins the end goal.
Leg Lifts 4 12 Can put your hands under your butt if you need the support. Hover your heels just off the floor, bring feet up to just below your center. Don't let your feet touch the ground or rest directly above center.
Seated Crunches 4 12 Support yourself at the end of a chair or bench. Feet off the ground bring your knees in and out.
90's Hold 2 AMRAP Not as hard as plank. Lay on your back. Tuck your tailbone so there is no gap between your back and the floor. Bring your knees up, with calves parallel to the floor so your legs make a 90 deg angle and hold. The trick, is to not let your back arch off the floor and maintain your pelvic tilt.
